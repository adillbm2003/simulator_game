1. Project Overview

The Bermuda Home Energy Simulator is an interactive web-based educational tool built for the Bermuda Department of Energy. It allows residents and students to simulate their home energy consumption using real 2026 BELCO electricity tariff rates, explore solar and battery options, and generate personalised energy reports.

Every user who accesses the simulator is required to register before use. Registration data is captured automatically and stored in a secure cloud database, giving the Department full visibility into who is using the tool and how.

Key Features

  • 18-appliance home energy simulation with BELCO 2026 tiered tariff rates
  • Solar PV + battery storage mode with net metering calculation
  • 30-day animated simulation playback
  • Efficiency grade scoring (A–F) with monthly bill estimates
  • PDF report export and CSV data download
  • Mandatory registration popup — captures name, email, institution, age, gender, learner level, and area of interest
  • Real-time admin dashboard for monitoring all registrations and sessions
